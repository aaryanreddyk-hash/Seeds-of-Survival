Welcome to Seeds of Survival

Crop diversity is the foundation of global food security. Yet thousands of traditional and wild crop species are disappearing due to monoculture farming, climate change, habitat loss, and modern industrial agriculture.

This website helps you explore the crisis facing endangered crop species and learn how you can take action.
