Volunteering Opportunities

    WWOOF (World Wide Opportunities on Organic Farms)

    WWOOF aims to connect people with organic farmers around the world in order to promote sustainable agriculture, cultural exchange, and environmentally friendly farming practices.

    Volunteer World – Forestry Work at an Ecovillage (Iceland)

    This program aims to support reforestation and environmental restoration by involving volunteers in forestry work at an eco-village focused on sustainable living and nature conservation.

    Volunteer World – Agriculture Work at a Community School (Nepal)

    This initiative aims to support local education and food production by helping maintain school gardens and agricultural activities that benefit students and the surrounding community.
