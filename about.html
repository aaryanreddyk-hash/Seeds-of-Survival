About Endangered Crop Species

Who

Farmers, scientists, conservationists, and global communities are committed to preserving crop diversity.

What

Endangered crop species are plants at risk of extinction due to climate change, habitat loss, and industrial agriculture.

When

The decline of crop species has accelerated dramatically over the past century.

Where

Many endangered crops trace back to ancient agricultural regions across Africa, Asia, the Middle East, and South America.

Why

We rely on diverse crops for food security, medicine, culture, and ecosystems.

How

Through seed banks, sustainable farming, research, public policy, and community involvement.
